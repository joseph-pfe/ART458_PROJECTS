The way I view it, my journey into the world of visual communication began fully in the 6th grade, experimenting with the narrative potential of digital slideshows and animation. This curiosity developed through years of creative exploration in band, interior design, and photography, eventually leading me to Graphic Design which I would start taking classes in during Fall 2022. I view design and creative direction to be a vast and interconnected field as a whole, and try to be a versatile creator who refuses to be boxed into a single niche.

My design aesthetic sits at the intersection of organization and playfulness, which I am constantly re-contextualizing and developing. I strive for work that is not only clean, structured, and conscious, but also a process that reflects those ideals. In this idea I make sure to retain a sense of fun and creativity that connects with the viewer on a human level. Whether ideating concepts on paper to find the perfect solution or executing high-fidelity layouts, my goal is to influence and inform through intentional visuals.

I consider myself an expert in the Adobe Creative Suite (Illustrator, Photoshop, InDesign) with emerging skills in UI/UX design using Figma and VS Code, and motion graphics with After Effects. Beyond just education, my experiences working with University Recreation has sharpened my ability to communicate with diverse audiences in a fast-paced environment while also in a team-setting. I am also a firm believer in the importance of mental well-being and work-life boundaries; with which I can approach every project with a refreshed perspective, ensuring that my creativity remains sustainable and my output remains impactful. I’m also a competent speaker of Castilian Spanish and make sure to bring my own identities and views to whatever I create.
